How to use?

We will show you each feature of our website!
Feature 1

Create an account

If you would like to use the full functionalities of our website, you are welcome to register an account and then log in. When you log in successfully, you can check your own game collections.

Feature 2

Find your Game

You can search for games by keyword or by game types and see game details. You can discuss with other users and add your own reviews.

Feature 3

Join the Community (Under developed)

You can now use the Community function to search the posts by other users and find most recent posts.

Feature 4

Find Friends (Coming soon)

You can search for friends by id or name, or you can choose to turn on location to find friends nearby. We will prioritise your friends by the similarity of your game collections.
